About RefiDoc

RefiDoc is an intelligent document assistant powered by Azure OpenAI. Upload internal documents (PDF/TXT), index them with embeddings using LangChain + FAISS, and chat with an AI assistant trained on your content.

💡 Inspiration

RefiDoc was born out of the need to give teams, especially in support centers and internal departments, quick access to organizational knowledge. Instead of searching manually through folders or documents, users can chat with their data.

🧩 What It Solves
  • ⚡ Fast answers from internal documents
  • 🔒 Secure search and retrieval
  • 🧠 Conversational AI trained on your files
  • 📂 Centralized knowledge for agents and teams
🧠 Technologies Used
  • Azure OpenAI – For chat completions and text embeddings
  • LangChain – Document loaders, chunking, and prompt construction
  • FAISS – Fast vector-based document retrieval
  • Flask + Jinja – Frontend application
  • FastAPI – Backend for document processing and API endpoints
  • SQLite – Local database for users and chat sessions
🌍 Recognition

RefiDoc was built for the Microsoft AI Hackathon 2025 as a demonstration of how powerful Azure OpenAI can be in real-world organizational workflows.
